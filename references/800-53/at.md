The Awareness and Training family addresses the need to ensure that personnel are adequately trained and aware of security and privacy risks, and that they understand their responsibilities in protecting organizational systems and information.

---

## AT-1: Policy and Procedures

Requires the organization to develop, document, disseminate, and periodically review and update awareness and training policy and procedures. The policy must address purpose, scope, roles, responsibilities, management commitment, coordination among organizational entities, and compliance.

**Key Enhancements:** None defined.

**Related Controls:** PM-9, PS-8, SI-12

**Baselines:** Low, Moderate, High

---

## AT-2: Literacy Training and Awareness

Requires the organization to provide security and privacy literacy training to system users (including managers, senior executives, and contractors) as part of initial training for new users, when required by system changes, and at an organization-defined frequency thereafter. Training content must include recognition and reporting of potential indicators of insider threat, social engineering, and other cyber threats.

**Key Enhancements:**

| # | Title |
|---|-------|
| AT-2(1) | Practical Exercises |
| AT-2(2) | Insider Threat |
| AT-2(3) | Social Engineering and Mining |
| AT-2(4) | Suspicious Communications and Anomalous System Behavior |
| AT-2(5) | Advanced Persistent Threat |
| AT-2(6) | Cyber Threat Environment |

**Related Controls:** AC-3, AC-17, AC-22, AT-3, AT-4, CP-3, IA-4, IR-2, IR-7, IR-9, PL-4, PM-13, PM-21, PS-7, PT-2, SA-8, SA-16

**Baselines:** Low, Moderate, High

---

## AT-3: Role-Based Training

Requires the organization to provide role-based security and privacy training to personnel with assigned security roles and responsibilities before authorizing access to the system or performing assigned duties, when required by system changes, and at an organization-defined frequency thereafter. Training must address the specific roles and responsibilities of the individual.

**Key Enhancements:**

| # | Title |
|---|-------|
| AT-3(1) | Environmental Controls |
| AT-3(2) | Physical Security Controls |
| AT-3(3) | Practical Exercises |
| AT-3(5) | Processing Personally Identifiable Information |

**Related Controls:** AC-3, AC-17, AC-22, AT-2, AT-4, CP-3, IR-2, IR-4, IR-7, IR-9, PL-4, PM-13, PM-23, PS-7, PS-9, SA-3, SA-8, SA-11, SA-16, SR-5, SR-6, SR-11

**Baselines:** Low, Moderate, High

---

## AT-4: Training Records

Requires the organization to document and monitor individual system security and privacy training activities, including basic awareness training, role-based training, and specific training. Retain training records for an organization-defined time period.

**Key Enhancements:** None defined.

**Related Controls:** AT-2, AT-3, CP-3, IR-2, PM-14, SI-12

**Baselines:** Low, Moderate, High

---

## AT-5: Contacts with Security Groups and Associations

*Withdrawn.* Incorporated into PM-15.

---

## AT-6: Training Feedback

Requires the organization to provide feedback on organizational training results to designated personnel at an organization-defined frequency. This supports continuous improvement of the training program based on effectiveness metrics and participant feedback.

**Key Enhancements:** None defined.

**Related Controls:** None

**Baselines:** None (not in any baseline)
