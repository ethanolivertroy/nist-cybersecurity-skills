# NIST SP 800-53A Rev 5 — AT: Awareness and Training Assessment Procedures

Assessment procedures for the Awareness and Training family evaluate whether the organization provides adequate security awareness training to personnel, role-based training to those with security responsibilities, and maintains training records.

---

## AT-1: Policy and Procedures

**Assessment Objective:** Determine if the organization develops, documents, disseminates, reviews, and updates awareness and training policy and procedures.

**Examine:** Awareness and training policy; awareness and training procedures; system security plan; organizational policy development documentation.

**Interview:** Organizational personnel responsible for awareness and training policy development and implementation.

**Test:** Organizational processes for managing awareness and training policy and procedures.

**Determination Statements:**
- AT-01a.[01] — An awareness and training policy is developed and documented
- AT-01a.[02] — The awareness and training policy addresses purpose, scope, roles, responsibilities, management commitment, coordination, and compliance
- AT-01b. — The awareness and training policy is disseminated to organization-defined personnel or roles
- AT-01c. — Awareness and training procedures to facilitate implementation of the policy are developed, documented, and disseminated
- AT-01d. — The awareness and training policy and procedures are reviewed and updated at an organization-defined frequency

---

## AT-2: Literacy Training and Awareness

**Assessment Objective:** Determine if the organization provides security and privacy literacy training to system users.

**Examine:** Awareness and training policy; security awareness training materials; training records; security awareness training curriculum; insider threat awareness training materials.

**Interview:** Organizational personnel who receive security awareness training; organizational personnel responsible for developing and delivering training.

**Test:** Automated mechanisms for managing awareness training; training tracking systems.

**Determination Statements:**
- AT-02a.[01] — Security and privacy literacy training is provided to system users as part of initial training for new users
- AT-02a.[02] — Security and privacy literacy training is provided when required by system changes
- AT-02a.[03] — Security and privacy literacy training is provided at an organization-defined frequency thereafter
- AT-02b. — Literacy training content includes recognition and reporting of potential indicators of insider threat
- AT-02c. — Security and privacy literacy training is updated at an organization-defined frequency or when changes to the threat environment require

---

## AT-3: Role-Based Training

**Assessment Objective:** Determine if the organization provides role-based security and privacy training to personnel with assigned security roles and responsibilities.

**Examine:** Awareness and training policy; role-based training materials; training records; role-based training curriculum; system security plan; personnel records identifying security roles.

**Interview:** Organizational personnel with security roles and responsibilities; training developers; system administrators.

**Test:** Automated mechanisms for managing role-based training; training tracking systems; practical exercise environments.

**Determination Statements:**
- AT-03a.[01] — Role-based security and privacy training is provided before authorizing access to the system or performing assigned duties
- AT-03a.[02] — Role-based security and privacy training is provided when required by system changes
- AT-03a.[03] — Role-based security and privacy training is provided at an organization-defined frequency thereafter
- AT-03b. — Role-based training content is defined based on assigned roles and responsibilities

---

## AT-4: Training Records

**Assessment Objective:** Determine if the organization documents and monitors security and privacy training activities and retains training records.

**Examine:** Awareness and training policy; training records; training attendance records; training certificates; system security plan.

**Interview:** Organizational personnel responsible for maintaining training records.

**Test:** Training record management systems; automated training tracking mechanisms.

**Determination Statements:**
- AT-04a. — Security and privacy training activities are documented and monitored
- AT-04b. — Individual training records are retained for an organization-defined time period
